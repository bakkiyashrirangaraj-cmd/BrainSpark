## 1. Content Strategy

### 1.1 Content Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Age-Appropriate** | Vocabulary and concepts match developmental stage |
| **Curiosity-Driven** | Questions that spark wonder, not test knowledge |
| **Open-Ended** | No single "correct" answer; exploration valued |
| **Safe** | Avoids scary, violent, or inappropriate themes |
| **Inclusive** | Represents diverse perspectives and experiences |

### 1.2 Content Mix

| Content Type | AI-Generated | Human-Curated | Notes |
|--------------|--------------|---------------|-------|
| Brain Sparks | 70% | 30% | Curated for quality, AI for variety |
| Follow-up Questions | 90% | 10% | AI excels at contextual follow-ups |
| Topic Introductions | 20% | 80% | Curated for consistency |
| Celebrations | 0% | 100% | All pre-written |
| Hints | 50% | 50% | Mix for variety |

#### Space

```yaml
topic:
  id: space
  name: Space
  category: science
  icon: rocket
  color: "#4B0082"

  introduction:
    wonder_cubs: |
      Look up at the sky tonight! See all those twinkly lights?
      Those are stars, just like our Sun, but really far away!
      Space is like a giant playground with planets, moons, and stars!

    curious_explorers: |
      Welcome to the universe - the biggest thing that exists!
      It's so big that light takes years to travel between stars.
      Ready to explore planets, black holes, and galaxies?

    mind_masters: |
      Space is 13.8 billion years old and contains over 2 trillion galaxies.
      We've only directly observed about 5% of the universe - the rest is
      dark matter and dark energy. What mysteries await?

  subtopics:
    - name: Planets
      depth_content:
        level_1: "Our solar system has 8 planets that orbit the Sun!"
        level_2: "Mercury is closest to the Sun, Neptune is farthest."
        level_3: "Jupiter is so big that 1,300 Earths could fit inside it!"
        level_4: "Some planets have dozens of moons - Jupiter has 95!"
        level_5: "Exoplanets are planets around other stars. We've found over 5,000!"

    - name: Stars
      depth_content:
        level_1: "Stars are giant balls of hot, glowing gas!"
        level_2: "Our Sun is a star - a medium-sized yellow one."
        level_3: "Stars are born in nebulae - giant clouds of gas and dust."
        level_4: "When big stars die, they explode as supernovae."
        level_5: "Neutron stars are so dense that a teaspoon weighs a billion tons!"

    - name: Moon
      depth_content:
        level_1: "The Moon lights up our night sky!"
        level_2: "The Moon changes shape because we see different parts lit by the Sun."
        level_3: "Only 12 humans have ever walked on the Moon."
        level_4: "The Moon is slowly moving away from Earth - 3.8cm per year."
        level_5: "The Moon was likely formed when a Mars-sized planet hit Earth!"

    - name: Black Holes
      min_age_group: curious_explorers
      depth_content:
        level_1: "Black holes have gravity so strong that even light can't escape!"
        level_2: "They form when giant stars collapse at the end of their lives."
        level_3: "There's a supermassive black hole at the center of our galaxy."
        level_4: "Time moves slower near a black hole due to gravitational time dilation."
        level_5: "Hawking radiation suggests black holes slowly evaporate over time."
```

## 7. Content Guidelines

### 7.1 Language Guidelines by Age

| Guideline | Wonder Cubs | Curious Explorers | Mind Masters |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Sentence length | 5-10 words | 10-20 words | Up to 30 words |
| Vocabulary | Kindergarten | Elementary | Middle school |
| Flesch-Kincaid | Grade 0-1 | Grade 2-5 | Grade 6-9 |
| Abstract concepts | Avoid | Introduce gently | Embrace |
| Rhetorical questions | Avoid | Occasional | Frequent |
| Humor | Silly, slapstick | Wordplay, puns | Wit, irony |

### 7.2 Forbidden Content

**Never include in ANY age group:**
- Violence or harm
- Scary or disturbing imagery
- Death (except age-appropriate: dinosaur extinction, etc.)
- Political opinions
- Religious judgment
- Discrimination of any kind
- Personal information requests
- External links or ads
- Brand promotions

### 7.3 Sensitive Topic Handling

| Topic | Wonder Cubs | Curious Explorers | Mind Masters |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Death | Avoid | "No longer alive" | Can discuss philosophically |
| Conflict | Avoid | Historical context only | Discuss causes/resolution |
| Feelings | Normalize all feelings | Discuss coping strategies | Explore psychology |
| Differences | "Everyone is special" | Celebrate diversity | Discuss social dynamics |
